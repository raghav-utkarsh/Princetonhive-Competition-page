Princeton Hive offers you opportunities to showcase your skills and compete at a global level, while solving problems related to the UN SDGs. Win awards and gain recognition amongst your peers.

Are you ready for a global skills competition that showcases your innovative solutions?
(Accepting entries from October 2020)





Who can participate ?

  • Participant under 18 are invited to enroll in the challenge.
  • Teachers can nominate student submissions.



Guidelines for the PSA

  • The PSA video should be no more than 2 minutes in length.
  • All the information presented in the video needs to be accurate and current.
  • No copyrighted materials are permitted to be used in your video.
  • No videos that show inaccurate or inapproriate information will be accepted.
  • All videos must be presented in English.
  • Entries will be considered complete when all materials have been received and acknowledged.
  • Entrants agree to grant Princeton Hive non-exclusive rights to use video entries and portions thereof.
  • All required forms and permission forms need to be completed and submitted. 

Students’ Step-by-Step Guide to FLIGHT!



Take-Off: AI Screening

All submitted videos will go through the Princeton Hive AI Scoring which will evalute your video on the following weighted parameters.

  • The use of specific terminology for the chosen Sustainable Development Goal.
  • Your face should be visible for most of the video presentation.
  • The presenter on video should demonstrate:
  • Enthusiasm in body language and voice
  • Consistent eye-contact
  • Appropriate language
  • Appropriate pacing

IN Flight: Final Judging

The Top 20 videos that successfully pass the AI Screening will become eligible for Final Judging.
Each video will be judged on creativity of ideas that best demonstrate the represented SDG. 
Entries will be considered complete when all materials have been received and acknowledged.
Entrants agree to grant Princeton Hive non-exclusive rights to use video entries and portions thereof.

Winner will be notified by Princeton Hive.
